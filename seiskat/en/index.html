In each of Helsinki, Oulu and Turku, a regional mathematics competition is organized for the 7th graders. The competition is not arranged in Satakunta in 2025. The purpose of the competitions is to provide challenge and to encourage the pupils to take mathematics as a hobby. The top contestants are also invited to participate in mathematics training.

N.B.: Due to the GDPR regulation the parents of the participants need to read the privacy policy and permit us to handle the contestants’ personal information; please see details.
